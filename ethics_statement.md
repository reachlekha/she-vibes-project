**Ethics Statement**

The KB Analyzer Agent is designed to support knowledge quality improvement while keeping humans in control of all critical decisions. The system provides recommendations for broken links, outdated product names, and non-inclusive language, but all suggested changes require human review and approval before implementation.

This project prioritizes responsible AI usage, data privacy, transparency, and fairness. Sensitive organizational or customer data should not be exposed to external AI systems without proper authorization and safeguards. The analyzer is intended to assist human decision-making, not replace it.

The project also acknowledges the possibility of inaccurate or biased AI-generated suggestions. Human validation remains mandatory to ensure contextual accuracy, inclusivity, and ethical content management practices.
